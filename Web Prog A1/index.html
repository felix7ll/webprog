Welcome

Welcome to Life – Living It Fully Everyday – we hope you enjoy your stay.



Due to the ongoing worldwide pandemic and the social policies it has resulted in it is not out of the ordinary for you to be feeling more anxious, and isolated than normal. If you haven’t been feeling your usual self this website may be able to help you to feel better in these tough times, through the appliation of various health and wellbeing exercises and lifestyle changes. Feel free to pick and sort the exercises and habits you prefer and ignor the rest, although they all benefit your health and wellbeing, whats more important is applying ones that you enjoy so that you may build a habit from them, and not become overwhelmed by the information on this website.

Why Life?

Why should you adopt health and wellbeing habits?



No matter your reason for beginning your practice of wellness or your experience with these exercises, Life will benefit you and help to reduce and alleviate symptoms of anxiety and isolation and lift your spirits through a variety of practices that are shown to have significant impact on an individuals wellbeing. The exerxercises promoted by Life will not only help you through this current stage of your life and through the chaos of the modern world, but will lay the foundation for habits to carry forward with you for the rest of your life, allowing you to live a healthier and happier life.

Our Mission

Life was created to help individuals manage their stress, and through the application of wellness exercises mould them into more mindful and productive individuals. In the information age we live in it is not uncommon to struggle to find answers on ‘how to mitigate stress’, or ‘which meditation technique should I use?.’ Life aims to give users and relaxed user experience having gathered all the necessary information and displaying in the appropriate pages. Having compiled the neccesary information for users, this can reduce the burden of doing your own research on these topics to which the search results would be countless. Users can rest easy as the infromation the want has been compiled for them and is at their fingertips.
